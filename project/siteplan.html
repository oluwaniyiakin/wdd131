Site Plan: TechForAll

Site Name: TechForAll - A website that focuses on making technology accessible to everyone. The name was selected to emphasize the mission of breaking barriers to learning tech, especially for beginners and those from underserved communities.

Site Purpose

The purpose of TechForAll is to provide easy-to-follow tutorials, resources, and news about technology. The site will include articles on programming, cybersecurity, and hardware reviews aimed at beginner to intermediate learners. It will also host a membership sign-up form for community engagement and newsletter subscriptions.

Scenarios

  • What are the best programming languages for beginners?
  • Where can I find a list of the latest affordable tech gadgets?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #1976d2 (used for headings, buttons, and accents)

Secondary Color: #f9f9f9 (used for background and body content)

Typography

Font Family: Arial, sans-serif (used for all text on the site)
